Our Story

Ayufresh was born from a simple belief — nature has already provided everything we need for perfect oral health. Our ancestors knew this. Ayurveda has taught this for thousands of years.

We created a botanical oral care product that combines the best of traditional Ayurvedic wisdom with modern formulation. Every ingredient is carefully sourced and every batch crafted with care.

Ayufresh Botanical Oral Care is a natural herbal mouth protector formulated with Clove, Neem, Custard Apple Leaf, Potassium Alum, Rock Salt and Purified Water — no harsh chemicals, ever.
