At Ayufresh, customer satisfaction is our priority. Please read our refund and cancellation policy carefully before placing an order.

1. Order Cancellation

a) Cancellation by Customer:

  • You may cancel your order within 12 hours of placing it, provided the order has not been shipped.
  • To cancel, contact us at ayufresh6@gmail.com with your order number and reason for cancellation.
  • If cancellation is requested before shipping, a full refund will be processed.
  • Once the order has been dispatched, cancellation is not possible. You may return the product after delivery as per our return conditions below.

b) Cancellation by Ayufresh:

We reserve the right to cancel an order due to:

  • Product unavailability or stock issues
  • Pricing or technical errors on the Website
  • Suspected fraudulent or unauthorized transactions
  • Incomplete or unverifiable customer information

In such cases, a full refund will be issued to you.

2. Return Conditions

We accept returns only under the following conditions:

  • Damaged Product: The product was damaged during shipping.
  • Wrong Product: You received a different product than what was ordered.
  • Manufacturing Defect: The product has a visible manufacturing defect (leakage, broken seal, etc.).
Returns NOT Accepted For

• Change of mind or personal preference

• Products that have been opened, used, or tampered with (unless defective)

• Products returned after 7 days from the delivery date

3. How to Request a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at ayufresh6@gmail.com within 48 hours of delivery.
  2. Include your order number, reason for return, and clear photos of the product and packaging.
  3. Our team will review your request and respond within 24–48 hours.
  4. If approved, we will arrange a pickup or provide return shipping instructions.

4. Refund Process

ScenarioRefund AmountTimeline
Order cancelled before shipping100% refund5–7 business days
Damaged / wrong product received100% refund or free replacement7–10 business days
Manufacturing defect100% refund or free replacement7–10 business days
Return approved after inspection100% of product value (shipping non-refundable if any)7–10 business days after product received back

5. Refund Method

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method used at the time of purchase:

  • UPI / Net Banking: Refund to the same bank account.
  • Credit / Debit Card: Refund to the same card (may take 5–10 business days depending on your bank).
  • Wallet Payments: Refund to the same wallet.

All refunds are processed through Razorpay. Razorpay's processing timeline applies.

6. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • The product has been opened and used (unless defective).
  • The return request is made after 7 days from delivery.
  • The product was damaged due to misuse or improper storage by the customer.
  • The customer provided an incorrect address leading to non-delivery and return-to-origin, where re-shipment is declined.

7. Exchange Policy

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to receive a different product or quantity, please initiate a return (if eligible) and place a new order.
